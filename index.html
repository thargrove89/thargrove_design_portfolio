About Me

My name is Travis Hargrove and I'm a graphic designer located in Greenville, North Carolina. I currently attend the University of East Carolina where I study information technology. I will be graduating in the spring of 2022. I have studied all facets of web development and design due to my curriculum at East Carolina University. From backend technologies like Nodejs and SQL to frontend technologies HTML, CSS, and Javascript. However, I specialize in graphic design. I love being creative and I believe graphic design combines art with information technology, which are my biggest passions.

I spend alot of my free time using Adobe photoshop and illustrator to make logos, edit photos and design mockups for my friends and family to keep my skills sharp. I love creating visually interesting things using photos, color, shape, and typography. All of my designs are simple, clean, and straight to the point. I can design a wide range of products like web banners, web templates, web advertisments, logos, and posters. When I am not designing or researching the latest design trends, I enjoy spending time with my family, watching movies, and reading biographies about great people in history.
